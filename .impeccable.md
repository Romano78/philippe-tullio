# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Film industry clients, production companies, brands, and collaborators evaluating a director for their next project. They arrive to be convinced by the work — not sold to. The site's job is **validation through atmosphere**. Secondary audience: festival programmers, peers, press.

### Brand Personality
**The Digital Auteur** — Cinematic · Atmospheric · Uncompromising

The interface is a cinematic frame, not a container. It communicates through mood, tension, and the raw power of moving images. Confident silence over noise.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Total Black philosophy** — depth through light and texture, not color
- Background `#131313` (not pure black), surfaces layered via `#0E0E0E` / `#1B1B1B` / `#2A2A2A`
- **Lime accent** `#B8FF00` used surgically — interaction states, critical callouts only
- **Intentional asymmetry** — no 50/50 splits, vast breathing voids, eye forced toward visual content
- **Typography as structure** — massive condensed headings feel like film titles (Space Grotesk / Aeonik Condensed), monospaced metadata (Aeonik Fono / Space Grotesk), Inter for body
- **Glassmorphism** on nav — `surface-container-highest` at 70% opacity, 32px backdrop blur
- **No 1px borders** — ever. Section boundaries via bg color shifts only
- **Ghost borders** only where unavoidable — `outline-variant` at 15% opacity max
- Ambient shadows: `on-surface` at 4% opacity, 40px blur, 0px offset

### Design Principles
1. **Imagery first** — high-quality visuals take 80–100% viewport width, UI gets out of the way
2. **No lines** — space and background shifts define hierarchy, never borders
3. **Editorial tension** — extreme typographic scale contrast between massive headings and small metadata creates cinematic rhythm
4. **Atmospheric depth** — layered surfaces simulate studio lighting falloff, not app-style elevation
5. **Restraint is power** — the lime accent earns its impact by being rare; clutter is the enemy of mood
